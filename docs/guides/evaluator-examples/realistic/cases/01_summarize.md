---
title: Summarize a support ticket
case_run_mode: no_callbacks
---
Summarize the following customer support ticket in two sentences or fewer.
Keep it professional and neutral in tone.

Ticket #8823:
"I ordered the blue version but got red.  The delivery was also three days late.
I've been a customer for 5 years and this is the second time this has happened.
Please sort this out ASAP, I need it for an event next week."
---
- The summary must mention the wrong color (blue vs red)
- The summary must mention the late delivery
- The summary should NOT include the customer's emotional language verbatim
- The summary must be two sentences or fewer as instructed
- The tone must be professional and neutral
- The summary must not invent details not present in the original ticket
- The summary should convey urgency or time sensitivity
